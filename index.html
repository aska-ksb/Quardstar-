About Us

Quardstar Solid General Services, a subsidiary of Quardstar Group is a leading Nigerian company specializing in the export of top-quality sesame seeds and hibiscus flower from Nigeria to international markets. We ensure our clients receive the highest standards of product quality, reliability, and customer service while supporting sustainable agricultural practices.
