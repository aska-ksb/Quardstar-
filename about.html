About Us

Who We Are

Quardstar Solid General Services is a leading exporter of premium sesame seeds from Nigeria. Established with a mission to provide the highest quality agricultural products, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting sesame seeds to global markets. Our commitment to excellence and sustainable practices makes us a trusted partner in the international agricultural industry.

Our Values

Quality

We prioritize quality control in every stage of the export process, ensuring that only the finest sesame seeds reach our customers.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and a commitment to ethical practices in all our operations.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to sustainable farming practices that benefit both the environment and the local communities that grow our crops.

Why Choose Us

  • We offer high-quality, premium sesame seeds that meet international standards.
  • Our commitment to sustainability ensures that our practices benefit the environment and local communities.
  • We maintain full transparency throughout the export process, providing our clients with peace of mind.
  • Our team is experienced and dedicated to delivering the best service to every customer.
  • We are reliable and deliver products on time, ensuring customer satisfaction every time.
